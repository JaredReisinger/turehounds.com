Having grown up on a ranch, and still having ties to one, Barbara is no stranger to breeding and raising animals. She is the mastermind behind any breedings at Ture Hounds, carefully choosing a sire and dam whose attributes will complement each other.

{% endcall %} {% call bioCard("Jared Reisinger", "jared-and-disa.jpg") %}

Having grown up in the wilds of suburbia, Jared is not the breeding mastermind. His primary role is to help raise the puppies, ensuring they, and he, have a lot of fun and silliness.

{% endcall %}

We are members of several Basenji and all-breed clubs:

  • Basenji Club of America (national Basenji club)
  • Copper State Basenji Club (Phoenix/Tucson, AZ, area Basenji club)
  • Evergreen Basenji Club (Seattle, WA, area Basenji club)
  • Basenji Club of Northern New Jersey (northern New Jersey area Basenji club)
  • Scottsdale Dog Fanciers Association (Scottsdale, AZ, all-breed club)
