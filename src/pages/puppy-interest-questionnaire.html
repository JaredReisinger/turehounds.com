Do you understand that a Basenji must always be on a leash or in a securely fenced area at all times? When the hunting instincts kick in, they are unstoppable and are very likely to dash off in pursuit of game. Please don’t think that you can train a Basenji to stay in an unfenced area (like a park, or on a hike), either. Five thousand years of instinct cannot be curbed easily. Being struck by an automobile is one of the most common causes of death or injury for a Basenji.
Do you understand that a Basenji must be kenneled/secured when riding in a car at all times? See the Center for Pet Safety for further details about the benefits both from a distraction and a survivability point of view.
